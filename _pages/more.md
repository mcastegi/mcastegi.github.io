Whenever I am not doing research or teaching, I am also...

## Research Assistant - EMU LAB

Since 2024 I am involved in the Economic and Monetary Union Laboratory (EMU Lab), a collaborative initiative organized by prof. Giancarlo Corsetti and prof. Marco Buti aiming at dissecting and rethinking the EMU’s architecture in the light of present-day conditions in Europe and the global economy. Click [here](https://www.eui.eu/en/projects/emu-lab) for more information. 


## Treasurer - Galileian School Alumni Association

We are an alumni association that promotes research and cultural initiatives, engaging high school students and introducing them to academic research, the social sciences, and STEM fields. Click [here](https://www.alumniscuolagalileiana.it/) for more information.
